mysql 查询某一用户的排名，由于mysql没有rownum，查询排名就比较麻烦了，所以记录下

    SELECT count(1) AS rank_num，
      FROM tab_game_rank
     WHERE pnumber >= (SELECT pnumber
                         FROM tab_game_rank
                        WHERE uid = 3)

rank_num 排名

pnumber 排名字段

uid 用户id
